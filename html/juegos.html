SdA Propuesta para el alumnado

Life then, now and tomorrow.

Juegos

Rosco

2

You will play a vocabulary game using the alphabet from A to Z. Some definitions related to topics such as environment, education, society, technology, and equality are given to you.

  1. Read or listen the definition carefully.
  2. Say or write the correct word that matches it.
  3. The word must start with the correct letter.
  4. If you don’t know the answer, the answer will be provided in 9 seconds.
  5. Each correct answer gives you one point.

Try to get as many correct answers as possible!

"Ñ" is the wild card
