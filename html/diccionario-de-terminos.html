Diccionario de términos

Students' dictionary

🌍 ENVIRONMENT

  1. Environment 👉 The natural world around us (air, water, land, animals).💬 Example: We must take action to protect the environment from pollution
  2. Pollution👉 Dirty or harmful substances in nature. 💬 Example: Cars cause air pollution.
  3. Climate change👉 Long-term changes in temperature and weather.💬 Example: Climate change is a global problem.
  4. Recycle👉 To use things again instead of throwing them away.💬 Example: We recycle paper and plastic.
  5. Sustainable👉 Using resources in a way that does not harm the future.💬 Example: Solar energy is sustainable.
  6. Industrial Revolution👉 Period in history when factories and machines changed how people lived and worked.💬 Example: The Industrial Revolution increased pollution.
  7. Deforestation👉 Cutting down trees in large areas.💬 Example: In the past, deforestation was less controlled.
  8. Urbanization👉 Growth of cities and towns over time.💬 Example: Urbanization has increased pollution in cities.
  9. Fossil fuels👉 Natural energy sources like coal, oil, and gas.💬 Example: Fossil fuels were widely used in the past.
  10.  Environmental awareness👉 Understanding and caring about environmental problems.💬 Example: Today, environmental awareness is higher than before.
  11. Pollution increase👉 Growth of harmful substances in air, water, or land over time.💬 Example: Pollution increased after industrial development.
  12.  Climate change impact👉 Effects of climate change on nature and people.💬 Example: Climate change impact is stronger today than in the past.

 
🎓 EDUCATION

  1. Education👉 The process of learning at school or in life.💬 Example: Education is important for the future.
  2. Learn👉 To get knowledge or skills.💬 Example: We learn new things every day.
  3. Teach👉 To help someone learn.💬 Example: Teachers teach students.
  4. Online learning👉 Learning using the internet.💬 Example: Online learning is more common today.
  5. Skills👉 Abilities you can develop.💬 Example: Communication is an important skill.
  6. Educational reform👉 Changes made to improve the education system.💬 Example: Many countries have had educational reforms.

 
⚖️ EQUALITY

  1. Equality👉 When everyone has the same rights and opportunities.💬 Example: Equality is important in society.
  2. Rights👉 Basic things all people should have.💬 Example: Education is a human right.
  3. Respect👉 Treating people in a kind and fair way.💬 Example: We must respect others.
  4. Discrimination👉 Treating people unfairly because they are different.💬 Example: Discrimination is not acceptable.
  5. Gender equality👉 Men and women have the same opportunities.💬 Example: Gender equality is improving.
  6. Apartheid👉 A system in South Africa where people were separated by race.💬 Example: During apartheid, Black people had fewer rights.
  7. Segregation👉 Keeping groups of people apart because they are different.💬 Example: Segregation separated Black and white people.
  8. Racism👉 Believing that some races are better than others.💬 Example: Racism creates inequality.
  9. Discrimination👉 Treating people unfairly because of their race, gender, etc.💬 Example: Apartheid was based on discrimination.
  10. Human rights👉 Basic rights that all people should have.💬 Example: Apartheid violated human rights.
  11. Freedom👉 The right to live without unfair control.💬 Example: People fought for freedom.
  12. Justice👉 Fair treatment for everyone.💬 Example: Justice means equal rights.

 

 
💻 TECHNOLOGY

  1. Technology👉 Tools and machines that help us in daily life.💬 Example: Technology has changed communication.
  2. Internet👉 A global network to share information.💬 Example: We use the internet every day.
  3. Social media👉 Platforms to communicate online.💬 Example: Social media is very popular.
  4. Artificial Intelligence (AI)👉 Machines that can think and learn.💬 Example: AI will be important in the future.
  5. Device👉 A piece of technology (phone, tablet, computer).💬 Example: Smartphones are common devices.

 
👥 SOCIETY

  1. Society👉 A group of people living together.💬 Example: Society changes over time.
  2. Community👉 A group of people in the same place.💬 Example: Our school is a community.
  3. Lifestyle👉 The way people live.💬 Example: Lifestyle today is very fast.
  4. Culture👉 Traditions, beliefs, and customs.💬 Example: Every country has a different culture.
  5. Globalization👉 The world becoming more connected.💬 Example: Globalization connects countries.
